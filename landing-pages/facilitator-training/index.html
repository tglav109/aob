The Honest Truth

You felt that, didn't you?

That subtle shift while your breath followed the orb above. The small softening. The quiet that arrived without you reaching for it. That is what facilitators learn to create in others. Smaller, and more powerful, than most people think.

You have probably felt it before. In a session that cracked something open. In a moment when your own breath surprised you. Something in you knows you are meant to hold space for others.

But the questions are real. Am I experienced enough? Can I actually build a practice from this? What if I invest and it does not work out?

Those questions are not obstacles. They are the sign that you are taking this seriously. And every facilitator we have trained started exactly where you are now. With one breath, and a quiet pull they could not explain.

The Path

Your path from student to facilitator

Four levels of training. Tap any level to see what it contains, where it takes place, and how long it lasts.

Level One · Foundation

Alchemy Regulation Method

The first step toward becoming a facilitator. Learn the core tools to regulate the nervous system using the ARM framework — the same foundation every AoB student begins with.

You will come out of ARM with a daily practice, a framework for your own regulation, and the groundwork that Practitioner Advanced and Facilitator Training build on.

FormatAvailable online & in-person
DurationSelf-paced
IncludesDaily practice & framework

Level Two · Deepening

iJourney

A deep personal transformation journey. Fourteen weeks of recorded teachings paired with one-to-one mentor sessions. You do the inner work before you hold space for anyone else.

Covers the seven chapters of inner journey: the Call, the Inner Child, Shadow Work, Finding the Treasure, Atonement, the Return, and Opening the Book of Your Life.

FormatOnline with mentored sessions
Duration14 weeks
ARM graduatesYour ARM tuition is credited toward iJourney

Level Three · Certification

Facilitator Training

Full certification as an AoB facilitator. Nine modules of professional skills: facilitation technique, guiding breathwork sessions, trauma awareness, Mandala Breath, integration, and the business of breathwork.

Weekly live calls with your trainers, real-time Q&A, expert feedback on your case studies and practice sessions. Graduate at BreathCamp in Tuscany.

FormatOnline or live in-person
Duration8 months · 400 hours
AccreditationTriple accredited

Level Four · Immersion

BreathCamp

An immersive week at ASHA, weaving traditional healing with contemporary neuroscience. Consolidate everything you have learned. Lead real sessions. Practice hands-on with your cohort.

Walk away certified, with the confidence that comes from having done the work in your own body, with real people, in a place built for this.

FormatIn-person only
Duration7 days
LocationASHA, Tuscany
