The Calm Code is a GPBA-aligned training that introduces functional breathing and nervous system regulation. It does not cover conscious connected breathwork delivery, trauma catharsis, altered-state practices, or clinical diagnosis. Participants are encouraged to integrate what they learn within their own existing scope of practice.
