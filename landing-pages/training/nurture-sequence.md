# Nurture Sequence — Facilitator Training Interest

A 6-email value-driven nurture series for leads who submit the interest form on `/training`. The above-all CTA is **Book a Discovery Call**. Each email earns the next one.

**Audience trigger:** Form submission on the Facilitator Training landing page (GHL form `GXgyYQ4wG1W8zo0USmsh`).
**Sender:** Anthony Abbagnano (or "The Alchemy of Breath team" for emails 2 and 5).
**From email:** `hello@alchemyofbreath.com`
**Discovery call link (every email):** `{{custom_values.breathwork_facilitator_training__discovery_call_url}}`
**Tone:** Warm, grounded, second-person, no hype. Mirror the landing page voice.
**Format:** Plain text style (lightweight HTML wrap is fine), short paragraphs, single primary CTA per email.
**Frequency cap:** Stop the sequence the moment they book a discovery call (`event = call_booked`).

## Implementation notes

### Triggers and exits
- **Trigger:** New form submission on the Facilitator Training page (GHL form ID `GXgyYQ4wG1W8zo0USmsh`).
- **Hard exit:** A discovery call is booked (track via GHL workflow on the booking calendar `nqi9d8dd3lS6nCTgGIbU`).
- **Soft exit (pause):** Reply contains the word `pause` — pause for 180 days, then resume with a re-engagement email.
- **Unsubscribe:** Standard CAN-SPAM / GDPR unsubscribe footer on every email.

### Personalisation tokens
- `[first_name]` — required. If missing, fall back to "there".
- Optional dynamic blocks: source page, what they said in the form's open text field, time zone for Breathe the World CTA.

### Tracking
- UTM the discovery call link as `?utm_source=email&utm_medium=nurture&utm_campaign=ft_nurture&utm_content=email_[1-6]`.
- Goal events: discovery call booked, Fundamentals enrolment, Breathe the World RSVP, reply received.

### Performance benchmarks (industry-standard wellness/coaching)
- Open rate: 35-45% (this is a warm, opt-in list)
- Click rate to discovery call: aim for 8-12% per email
- Discovery call booking rate across the full sequence: 12-18% of leads

### Follow-on
After Email 6, move leads to a long-term **Community Newsletter** segment (monthly) so you stay in their inbox without overworking the welcome sequence. Re-engage with cohort-launch announcements when new intakes open.

### A/B test ideas
- **Email 1 subject:** "Welcome — your path starts here" vs "We got your application"
- **Email 4 subject:** "Why I built this training" vs "It started with one breath"
- **Email 6 subject:** "One last invitation" vs "Should I stop emailing you?"
- **CTA wording:** "Book your discovery call" vs "Talk to a Success Advisor"
