What this is

A complete training in breath and nervous system regulation.

You felt it with the orb above. A few breaths in, something shifted. That is not a trick. It is physiology. And most people who want to work with breath, in their own life or for the people they care for, already have the heart for it. What is often missing is the understanding of why it works, and the confidence to use it when it matters.

ARM is the proprietary methodology developed by Anthony Abbagnano and Amy Rachelle. Drawing on over two decades of breathwork practice, somatic science, and the direct results of working with thousands of people. It teaches how the body actually breathes, why patterns break down, and what to do about it. Simply, safely, and on demand.

The world does not get less chaotic.
ARM trains the system that has to live inside it.

The Curriculum

Four pillars.
One complete foundation.

What you actually study, in either format. The mechanics, chemistry, cadence, and application of breath as a tool for regulation.

01

Pillar One

Mechanics

How the body is designed to breathe, and what gets in the way. The structural foundations, explored hands-on.

  • Nasal breathing function and restoration
  • Diaphragm assessment and activation
  • Ribcage mobility and accessory breathing
  • Postural integration across positions
02

Pillar Two

Chemistry

Why over-breathing creates dysregulation, and why CO2 tolerance matters more than most people realise. Taught simply, with safety first.

  • CO2 tolerance and why it matters
  • Over-breathing pattern recognition
  • Anxiety physiology: the breath connection
  • Gas exchange and oxygen delivery basics
03

Pillar Three

Cadence

The rhythm and pacing of breath that shifts how the nervous system responds. You experience each practice in your own body first.

  • Coherence breathing and resonance
  • Core techniques: box breath, 4-7-8, transformer breath
  • How breath pacing affects the autonomic nervous system
  • Vagal tone and heart-rate variability
04

Pillar Four

Application

Where understanding becomes confidence. Through partner practice and feedback, you learn what it feels like to guide someone toward calmer breathing.

  • The 90-second rapid reset protocol
  • The 12-minute daily regulation practice
  • Breath assessment and partner practice
  • Holding space and the basics of coaching with breath

The Methodology

Six stages of regulation

The arc you move through across the training. The curriculum above is what you study. The six stages are the developmental shape of how it lands.

Stage One

Remember

Return to who you were before the dysregulation. Reconnect with the body's original intelligence. Beneath the noise, beneath the pattern.

The body remembers a baseline of safety, even when the mind has forgotten. Stage One is the work of finding that signal again.

Stage Two

Reframe

Change the story the nervous system is telling. Shift the meaning of past experience so the body stops bracing for a threat that has already passed.

Reframe is not positive thinking. It is the slow, somatic work of teaching the body that the danger is no longer in the room.

Stage Three

Resource

Build internal capacity. Fill the tank. Learn to draw on breath, body, and community as living sources of regulation. Available anywhere, any time.

A regulated nervous system is not a state you visit. It is a reserve you carry.

Stage Four

Regulate

Shift out of chronic activation. Learn the direct pathways from dysregulation to baseline. Through the breath, through the body, on demand.

This is the technical core of ARM. The protocols you can run on yourself in any moment.

Stage Five

Realign

Bring the body, mind, and purpose back into coherence. When the nervous system is settled, clarity returns. And with it, the right direction.

Realignment is the natural consequence of regulation. The signal beneath the static is finally audible.

Stage Six · The summit

Rise

Not just survival. Not just recovery. A robust nervous system becomes the foundation for genuine vitality, leadership, and an unshakeable life.

Rise is what becomes possible when the system can finally hold what life puts on it.

The Science

Measurable changes in the system that runs everything else.

ARM works directly with the body's autonomic nervous system. Through conscious breathwork, somatic practice, and structured regulation protocols, you build measurable improvements in:

The body's primary brake on the stress response.

Heart rate variability. The signature of a resilient system.

Cortisol

Calmer baseline. Faster return after stress.

Flexibility

Autonomic flexibility. The capacity to shift states with intention.

A trauma-informed foundation

ARM is the foundation that makes all other breathwork safer and more effective. The training is trauma-informed throughout. We work with the nervous system understanding trauma sits inside.

ARM does not teach conscious connected breathwork delivery, trauma catharsis, or somatic release. Those deeper modalities are taught in the AoB Facilitator Training, which ARM graduates can enter directly.
